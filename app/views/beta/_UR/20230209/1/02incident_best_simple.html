Incident Details

The content in these sections is exactly as the original reporter wrote it.

1. Why was the customer in contact with us?

To provide an update in the UC journal about the efforts at finding suitable work

2. How did the incident start? Was there a specific trigger?

I left a message in the UC asking about how the claimant was getting on with finding work and to provide an update. He gets upset when asked about finding work stating no one wants to employ him.

3. What were the issues with the customer's behaviour?

He responded by swearing – “ go and fuck yourself stupid cow”, “ I will come round there and bomb you lot to bits”. I find his comments sexist and unnecessary; the threat is something to be taken seriously.

4. Tell us what happened next. How did you try to resolve the situation, and how did the customer react?

I decided to report the comment as I find it unacceptable and do not feel comfortable communicating with the claimant. I informed my team leader I feel uncomfortable with the message and the threat needs to be addressed

5. Did any of your colleagues see or hear what happened?

No

6. How did your interaction with the customer end?

The interaction was via the journal, I did not respond to the claimant comments.

7. How did you feel during the incident?

I was uncomfortable reading the message and concerned about the threat being made.
